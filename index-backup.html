Financial Model Options

Option 1: Traditional

  • HSS bills/collects professional fee.
  • Hospital pays HSS an agreed yearly subsidy in monthly installments.

Option 2: WRVU

  • Hospital pays HSS an agreed price per wRVU/study.
  • HSS bills/collects professional fee and returns collections to hospital.

Option 3: Hospital Collection

  • Hospital pays HSS an agreed price per wRVU/study.
  • Hospital bills/collects and keeps professional fee.
